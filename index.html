01 About

Hi, I'm Avery.

A first-year Media Production student at Toronto Metropolitan University, actively building experience in concert photography, short-form social media video content, and behind-the-scenes photography for student film projects.

As a frequent concert-goer, my work focuses on capturing the energy, emotion, and atmosphere of live performances — telling a story through each image and video, and documenting authentic moments on stage and behind the scenes.
